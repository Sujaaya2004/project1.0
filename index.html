ABOUT US!!


Welcome to Banana Leaf, your gateway to authentic South Indian cuisine! Located in the heart of Coimbatore, Banana Leaf is dedicated to bringing you the rich and diverse flavors of South India, served on fresh banana leaves.

Our chefs, deeply rooted in South Indian culinary traditions, prepare each dish with the freshest ingredients and aromatic spices. Every bite is a celebration of our heritage, enhanced by the traditional use of banana leaves that enrich both flavor and experience.

Beyond our delicious food, we offer a warm and inviting atmosphere that reflects the vibrant culture of South India. Whether you're here for a family dinner, a festive celebration, or a quiet meal, we ensure a memorable dining experience.

Join us at Banana Leaf and embark on a culinary journey that honors the rich traditions of South Indian cuisine. We look forward to welcoming you and sharing our passion for authentic South Indian food.
